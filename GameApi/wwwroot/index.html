Welcome to ElectricGames

Welcome to Electric games, here is our functionality:

  • Games

    You can view all the games in our database.
    It is also possible to search for games by Id or game title.
    You get search feedback immediately with our live search.

  • Add or Delete

    Here you can add a new game with image and extra information
    There is also a delete function, where you enter the Id of game to delete it.

  • Update

    Here you can update the information of a game thats currently in our database.
    You enter the Id, then fetch the game you want to edit.
    The changes you make can be seen live. When the information you want is updated you press the update button
